Class implementing a Modal Transition of an Automaton. #lt;br#gt; A modal transition is a transition further equipped with a modality. #lt;br#gt; Modalities are either permitted and necessary. #lt;br#gt; A permitted transition is controllable. #lt;br#gt; Necessary transitions can be either urgent (i.e., uncontrollable) or lazy. #lt;br#gt; A lazy transition can be either controllable or uncontrollable according #lt;br#gt; to a controllability predicate that predicates over the set of transitions of an automaton. #lt;br#gt; <br/>@param #lt;S1#gt; generic type of the content of S<br/>@param #lt;L1#gt; generic type of the content of L<br/>@param #lt;S#gt; generic type of the state<br/>@param #lt;L#gt; generic type of the label<br/>
