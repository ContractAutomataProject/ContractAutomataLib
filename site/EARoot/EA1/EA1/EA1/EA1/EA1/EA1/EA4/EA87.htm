Class implementing the orchestration synthesis for a specific product of a product line. #lt;br#gt; This is a further specialization of the orchestration synthesis where the requirement also checks  #lt;br#gt; that an action must not be forbidden by the product, and in the resulting synthesised automaton   #lt;br#gt; all required actions must be reachable (otherwise an empty orchestration is returned).  #lt;br#gt;   This operation is formally specified in Definition 14 of<br/><ul>
<li>Basile, D. et al., 2020. Controller synthesis of service contracts with variability. Science of Computer Programming, vol. 187, pp. 102344. (<a href="file://https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scico.2019.102344">https://doi.org/10.1016/j.scico.2019.102344</a>)</li></ul> <br/>@param #lt;S1#gt; the type of the content of states <br/><br/>
