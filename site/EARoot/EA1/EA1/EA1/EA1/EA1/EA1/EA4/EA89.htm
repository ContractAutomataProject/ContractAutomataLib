Class implementing the relabeling operator.  #lt;br#gt; This operator can update the labels of states of an automaton, as well as initial and final states. #lt;br#gt; As a side effect, in case the relabeling is the identity function, a clone of an automaton is created.  #lt;br#gt; <br/>@param #lt;S1#gt; the generic type content of the states<br/>@param #lt;L#gt; the generic type of the label, constrained to be a sub-type of Label#lt;Action#gt;<br/><br/>
