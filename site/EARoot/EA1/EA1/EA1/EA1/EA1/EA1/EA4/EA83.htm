This class implements a model checking operation followed by a synthesis operation. #lt;br#gt; In case the property to model-check is not given, the synthesis operation is applied straightforward. #lt;br#gt; Otherwise, the synthesis operation is applied on the result of the application of the model checking function. #lt;br#gt; <br/>@param #lt;S1#gt; the generic type of the content of states<br/>@param #lt;S#gt; the generic type of states, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;State#lt;S1#gt;#lt;/code#gt;<br/>@param #lt;L#gt; the generic type of the labels of the automaton to check, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;L2#lt;/code#gt;<br/>@param #lt;T#gt; the generic type of the transitions of the automaton to check, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;ModalTransition#lt;S1,Action,S,L#gt;#lt;/code#gt;<br/>@param #lt;A#gt; the generic type of the automaton to check, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;Automaton#lt;S1,Action,S,T #gt;#lt;/code#gt;<br/>@param #lt;L2#gt; the generic type of the labels of the property, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;Label#lt;Action#gt;#lt;/code#gt;<br/>@param #lt;T2#gt; the generic type of the transitions of the property, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;ModalTransition#lt;S1,Action,S,L2#gt;#lt;/code#gt;<br/>@param #lt;A2#gt; the generic type of the automaton property, must be a subtype of #lt;code#gt;Automaton#lt;S1,Action,S,T2 #gt;#lt;/code#gt;<br/>
